How to add credit

Login

Create an account or Open your existing account from the top of the Home Page.

Wallet

Open your Wallet from the top of the Home Page - to the left of Login Button.

Deposit

Scan the QR or copy your wallet address to send Cryptocurrency to your wallet.

Confirmation

Once the amount is successfully transferred a confirmation will be sent to you via email or SMS.

Success

Now you can use your wallet balance to buy tickets and participate in upcoming draws.
